Interactive Ratio Practice

Calculate the three key ratios using the mock statement below. Enter answers to check your result.

MCDONALD'S FRANCHISE MOCK STATEMENT$ AUD
Total Sales Revenue$1,000,000
Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)$600,000
Gross Profit$400,000
Current Assets (Cash, Inventory)$150,000
Current Liabilities (Short-term debt)$50,000
Total Liabilities (Total Debt)$200,000
Total Equity (Owner's funds)$200,000

1. Liquidity Ratio (Current Ratio)

Current Assets ÷ Current Liabilities

2. Solvency / Gearing Ratio (Debt-to-Equity)

Total Liabilities ÷ Total Equity

3. Profitability Ratio (Gross Profit Ratio)

(Gross Profit ÷ Sales) × 100
