Paragraph Structure: Business PEEL Model

To score top marks, use this structural format for your body paragraphs:

Point (P)
Topic Sentence:

Directly state your argument answering the question, using specific syllabus terms.

Explain (E)
Detailed Analysis:

Analyse the cause-and-effect relationship. How or why does this occur? (e.g. transmission mechanism of consumer choice, or the cost of compliance).

Evidence (E)
Case Study Application:

Provide a concrete, real-world example of a business (e.g., Apple, McDonald's) applying this strategy or encountering this influence.

Link (L)
Business Outcome:

Link back to the question and state the ultimate effect on the business's goals (profitability, competitive advantage, sales volume, market share).
